About The Events:

The Karnavati University Music Fest is an annual event where students showcase their musical talents. Join us for a day filled with melodies, rhythms, and performances from different genres.

Event Highlights

Classical Night

Experience the magic of Indian Classical Music with talented students and guest performers.

Rock Band Competition

Cheer for your favorite bands as they battle it out with electrifying performances.

Solo Singing

Melodious solo singing performances by students from different courses.
